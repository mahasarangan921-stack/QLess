Quick Help
Frequently Asked Questions
Can't find your answer above? Browse common questions below.
How do I book a token? +
Go to the Home page and scroll to the "Book Your Token" section. Select a service center, pick a date and time, fill in your details, upload any required documents, and confirm. You'll get a token number instantly.
Can I cancel or reschedule my token? +
Yes! Go to My Tokens, find your active token and tap the Cancel or Reschedule option. Cancellations are free up to 1 hour before your slot.
How will I know when it's my turn? +
You'll receive a push notification and can see the live countdown on your Dashboard. The active token card updates every few seconds showing how many people are ahead of you.
Is QLess free to use? +
Yes, QLess is completely free for users. Create an account, book tokens, and skip queues at zero cost. Some premium features may be available in the future.
Which service centers are supported? +
QLess supports hospitals & clinics, banks, government offices, RTO offices, post offices, pharmacies, college admin offices, and restaurants. Use the Find Center search on the Home page to locate one near you.
What documents do I need to upload? +
Required documents depend on the service. Most centers require a government ID proof. RTO offices may require vehicle papers. You can upload PDF, JPG or PNG files via the Upload Docs section.
